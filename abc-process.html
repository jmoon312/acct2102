Step 5

What if Peach Lab expands Whole Bean SKUs?

activity volumes drive cost

Marketing wants to launch five new Whole Bean varieties (single-origin Ethiopian, Colombian decaf, holiday blend, etc.) without changing total batch volume. The new lineup means more changeovers and more QC work:

• Whole Bean setups rise from 30 to 60 per year (each new SKU needs clean changeovers).
• Whole Bean quality tests rise from 10 to 25 per year (each SKU gets its own test panel).
• All activity rates and other driver quantities stay the same.

Cold Brew is untouched.
