Step 1

Set the flexible budget

benchmark the standards against actual output

Copperline Candle Co. makes scented candles. The cost accountant built the month's plan from these per-candle standards: each candle should take 0.50 lb of wax at $4.00 per lb, 0.10 direct-labor hours at $15.00 per hour, and should absorb variable overhead at $8.00 per direct-labor hour.

The static (planning) budget assumed 5,000 candles. Production actually came in at 5,400 candles this month. Before pulling any variances, the accountant flexes the budget to what the standards say should have been spent at the 5,400-candle output level.
