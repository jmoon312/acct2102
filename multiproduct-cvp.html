Step 2

Weighted-average contribution margin

one CM/unit number for the whole company

Some of Jeni's units throw off only $0.40 of CM (bars), some throw off $16 (cakes). To talk about the company as one CVP problem, we need a single CM per unit that reflects the product mix — the weighted-average contribution margin (WACM).

Given the sales mix above, what is the weighted-average contribution margin per unit? Round to the nearest cent if needed.
