Company Information

Summit Pack Co. — budgeting for May

Summit Pack Co. manufactures one product, a trail backpack. The controller is preparing the company's master budget for May: the production budget, the direct materials and direct labor budgets, manufacturing overhead, and the unit cost that feeds cost of goods sold. Every fact she has is listed below. Pull the pieces you need for each step; not all of them belong in every schedule.

Direct materials (per backpack)

  • Ripstop fabric: 3 yards per unit at $4 per yard.
  • Webbing & hardware: 2 units per unit at $5 each.
  • Desired ending raw materials each month = 25% of next month's production needs.
  • On hand May 1: 4,650 yards of fabric and 3,100 units of hardware.

Direct labor & overhead

  • Direct labor: 0.5 hours per unit at $20 per hour.
  • Variable manufacturing overhead: $8 per direct labor hour.
  • Fixed manufacturing overhead: $31,000 per month (includes $6,000 depreciation).

Other

  • Prior-year May sales were 5,500 units.
  • The company employs 45 production workers.
  • A duffel-bag line was evaluated last year and discontinued.
