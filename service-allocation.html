Step 2

Step Method — pick the order

close the service dept that sends the most to other services first

Step method recognizes that service departments use each other's services. You allocate one service dept at a time, starting with the one whose services flow most heavily into other service departments. Once a dept is allocated, it's closed and receives no further allocations.

Use the operating data in Step 1 to figure out which service dept routes the most of its base into the other two service depts — that one goes first.

Set the allocation order. Each service department appears exactly once.
