BarkSpa

dog grooming

BarkSpa's monthly fixed costs are $2,400 (rent and depreciation on tubs and dryers). Its variable cost is $7 per groom (shampoo, treats, and direct labor at the standard pay rate).

The relevant range — the activity level where this cost behavior holds — is 100–500 grooms per month. If BarkSpa operates between 501 and 1,000 grooms in a month, two things change: an additional tub and dryer are needed, adding $1,200 to fixed costs, and the existing groomers have to work overtime, raising variable cost to $9.50 per groom.
